Process

From event to insight
in seconds.

Every alert passes through four precision stages — giving you context, not just noise.

01
Classification

The event type is identified — geopolitical tension, supply disruption, macro data release, or corporate news.

02
Asset Mapping

The event is mapped to the assets most likely to move — commodities, equities, indices, and sectors.

03
Impact Evaluation

A directional bias (bullish or bearish) and confidence level are assigned using historical context and real-time data.

04
Alert Generation

A concise, actionable alert arrives in real time — what happened, why it matters, which assets are affected, and the directional read.
