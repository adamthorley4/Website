# StockSight AI — Company Overview

## What StockSight AI Does

StockSight AI is a real-time market intelligence platform designed to identify and interpret events that are likely to impact financial markets before price movements are fully reflected.

The platform continuously monitors a wide range of data sources, including global news outlets, social media platforms (such as X/Twitter), economic data releases, and geopolitical developments. Its core function is to detect **signals** — pieces of information or events that have the potential to influence supply, demand, or overall market sentiment.

---

## How It Works

When a signal is detected, StockSight AI processes it through several stages:

1. **Classification** — The type of event is identified (e.g. geopolitical tension, supply disruption, macroeconomic data release, corporate news)
2. **Asset mapping** — The event is mapped to the relevant assets likely to be affected (commodities, equities, indices, sectors)
3. **Impact evaluation** — A directional bias (bullish or bearish) and confidence level are assigned based on historical patterns, contextual data, and real-time conditions
4. **Alert generation** — A concise, actionable alert is delivered to the user in real time

### Example Alert

> *"Iran threatens to close the Strait of Hormuz. Potential oil supply disruption — bullish for oil prices."*

Each alert includes:
- A short description of the event
- Why the event matters
- Which assets are likely to be affected
- A directional insight

---

## User Experience

The platform is built around **speed and clarity**. Users receive instant notifications via web or mobile, allowing them to quickly understand what is happening without needing to interpret raw news or complex data themselves. StockSight AI prioritises surfacing high-impact signals early, rather than overwhelming users with large volumes of information.

StockSight AI does not aim to guarantee predictions or provide financial advice. It focuses on early signal detection and contextual insight — helping users understand what is driving market movements before those movements become obvious or widely priced in.

## Positioning

> **"See the signal before the move."**

**Key themes:**
- Markets move on signals, not headlines
- The signal comes before the price move
- Most people react late because they only see the outcome
- StockSight AI focuses on the cause

**Simple one-line summary:**
StockSight AI detects market-moving signals in real time and explains their potential impact before the broader market fully reacts.

**Primary value proposition:**
StockSight AI helps users understand what is moving markets in real time, so they can react faster and make more informed decisions.

## Target Audience

- Retail traders and investors
- People interested in financial markets, macro trends, and news
- Users who feel they are often "late" to market moves

---

## Tone of Voice

- Intelligent, confident, and minimal
- Avoid hype, exaggeration, or "get rich quick" language
- Focus on insight, awareness, and understanding
- Never position as financial advice or guarantee of profit

---

## Marketing Messaging Guidelines

Emphasise **clarity, speed, and understanding** — not promises of guaranteed profits or perfect predictions.

**Use:**
- "Understand what's moving markets"
- "See the signal before the move"
- "React faster with real-time intelligence"
- "The cause, not just the outcome"

**Avoid:**
- Profit guarantees or performance claims
- Competitor comparisons
- Financial advice framing
- Hype or urgency-baiting language
